We introduce AEMDAS, a six-stage audit sequence comprising six disciplines: eigencartogrophonology, neuralography, interventionalmatonomies, interspectraloptimetrics, ontaxonomolographetics, and autographenlemnics. We define the cognitohazard: information whose topology makes every response except irrelevance feed the system. We identify the gatekeeper as the minimum cut of the information graph. Cross-domain analysis reveals r = +0.93 correlation between the I-80 event and Skinwalker Ranch phenomena.
