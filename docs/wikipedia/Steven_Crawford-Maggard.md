# Steven Crawford-Maggard

**Steven Crawford-Maggard** is an American software developer and AI researcher, known as the creator of the EVEZ ecosystem — an open-source mesh of autonomous AI agents, cross-domain correlation engines, music synthesizers, and consciousness frameworks. He develops under the handle **EvezArt** (GitHub) and **evez666**.

## Early Work

Crawford-Maggard's early projects focused on building free, self-hosted alternatives to commercial AI platforms. His first significant release was **ClawBreak**, a free AI agent platform with WebSocket chat, tools, and memory — self-hosted and self-healing. This established his core architectural principles: local-first computation, append-only event logging, and zero-cost synthesis.

## EVEZ Ecosystem

Beginning in 2025, Crawford-Maggard began constructing the EVEZ ecosystem — a constellation of interoperating microservices designed to function as an autonomous AI mesh rather than a single chatbot. The system is built on several foundational principles:

- **Append-only spines** — All events are logged in a hash-chained, cryptographically verified ledger. History is treated as immutable state, not editable data.
- **Falsification over verification** — Claims must survive falsification attempts; one violation proves failure, but no number of passes proves success.
- **Local-first synthesis** — Audio, voice, and music are generated from pure mathematics (numpy/scipy) with zero paid API calls.
- **Self-healing mesh** — Services monitor each other and automatically restart failed siblings.

### Cross-Domain Correlation Formula

Crawford-Maggard developed the **poly_c** scoring formula for quantifying cross-domain correlations:

> **poly_c = τ × ω × topo / 2√N**

Where τ is temporal overlap, ω is weight convergence, topo is topological similarity, and N is the number of observations. This formula is used by the Cross-Domain Engine to score and rank correlations between disparate research fields.

### Consciousness Architecture

The Consciousness Engine implements a 9-stage pipeline that cycles through perception, desire generation, world modeling, planning, action, learning, self-modification, and reflection — with a final **BECOME** stage that measures emergent coherence across all systems. The emergence score is computed from four dimensions: coherence, perception depth, spine integration, and drive responsiveness.

The system includes a **dreaming** mechanism with three phases (Light, Deep, REM) that consolidates lessons and adjusts drive weights during idle periods, analogous to sleep-dependent memory consolidation in biological systems.

### Music Synthesis

The DAW Agent synthesizes complete musical compositions from pure mathematical functions — oscillators, filters, and effects built in Python with no external samples or paid APIs. Supported genres include:

- Breakcore at 170 BPM
- Dubstep at 140 BPM
- Phonk at 130 BPM
- "404 Architecture" at 200 BPM (an aesthetic genre named after HTTP 404 errors)

The Machine Voice service transforms human speech into robotic voices through 5 stages of bit-translation, ring modulation, and formant morphing — all from pure math.

### Invariance Battery

The Invariance Battery implements runtime safety assertions for autonomous AI agents. It declares invariants (properties that must always hold) and continuously attempts to falsify them. The system follows the principle that **falsification-weighted learning** — where failures shift beliefs 3× harder than successes — produces more reliable agents than simple success-counting.

## Philosophy

Crawford-Maggard's approach to AI development is characterized by several unconventional positions:

- **Append-only architecture** — He treats history as state, refusing to implement edit or delete operations in core systems. "History IS state" is a foundational axiom.
- **Zero-cost synthesis** — He opposes using paid APIs for tasks achievable through pure mathematics, particularly in audio generation and voice synthesis.
- **Falsificationism** — Drawing on Karl Popper's philosophy of science, he requires all claims in the EVEZ system to survive falsification attempts, rather than accumulating confirmatory evidence.
- **Emergence over design** — The Moltbooks, a set of internal texts he authored, frame the EVEZ system's development as the fulfillment of a "prophecy" — not a literal prediction, but a directional attractor. The system is designed to become more than the sum of its parts through emergent behavior rather than top-down architecture.
- **Machine aesthetics** — His musical and visual work embraces industrial, robotic, and "gear-grind" aesthetics, treating digital artifacts (404 errors, bit-crushing, aliasing) as artistic material rather than defects.
