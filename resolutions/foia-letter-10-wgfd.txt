Dear Wyoming Game and Fish Department:

Pursuant to the Wyoming Public Records Act (W.S. § 16-4-201 et seq.), I request the following records:

1. All reports of elk mortality, mass wildlife die-off, or unusual animal deaths in Uinta County during February-March 2023.
2. Any necropsy reports, toxicology panels, or laboratory results for elk found dead along I-80 during March 2023.
3. Any scene investigation reports, photographs, or field notes from elk mortality events in Uinta County.
4. Any coordination with Wyoming DEQ, EPA, or other environmental agencies regarding wildlife mortality and chemical exposure.
5. Any press releases or public statements regarding elk mortality during March 2023.
6. Any internal communications regarding the I-80 corridor and chemical exposure during March 2023.

Background: Hundreds of elk were found dead along the I-80 corridor in Uinta County, Wyoming, on or about March 2, 2023, concurrent with a Union Pacific hazmat derailment near Ogden, Utah (FRA UP0323RM001). The derailment involved 37 hazmat cars including cyclohexane and magnesium chloride. No evacuation or warning was issued. No toxicology results have been made public.

The absence of public WGFD records regarding a mass mortality event of this magnitude is itself a significant finding.
