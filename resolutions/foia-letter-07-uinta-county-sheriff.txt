Pursuant to the Wyoming Public Records Act (W.S. § 16-4-201 et seq.), I request the following records:

1. All incident reports filed between March 1-15, 2023, involving Interstate 80 in Uinta County, Wyoming.
2. All reports involving elk mortality, wildlife mortality, or animal carcasses on or near I-80 during March 2023.
3. All reports involving chemical odor, hazardous material, plume, or air quality complaints along I-80 during March 2023.
4. All dispatch records and 911 call logs between March 1-5, 2023.
5. Any mutual aid requests from Union Pacific Railroad or Ogden, Utah agencies during March 2023.
6. Any reports involving Union Pacific Railroad incidents in Uinta County during February-March 2023.

Background: On or about March 2, 2023, a Union Pacific train derailment occurred near Ogden, Utah (FRA Record UP0323RM001) involving 37 hazmat cars including cyclohexane and magnesium chloride. I-80 was open to traffic at this time. Multiple motorists on I-80 in Uinta County reported a visible chemical plume. Hundreds of elk were found dead along the I-80 corridor. No evacuation or public warning was issued.

I am requesting these records as a witness to the event and as a concerned citizen. If any records are withheld, please cite the specific statutory exemption and provide a redacted version.
