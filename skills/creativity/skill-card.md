## Description: <br>
Generate novel ideas calibrated to user taste. Auto-learns preferred styles, risk levels, and creative directions through feedback. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[ivangdavila](https://clawhub.ai/user/ivangdavila) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Creators, writers, designers, and other users use this skill to generate idea options across safe, stretch, and wild directions, then refine future outputs based on recorded feedback and preferences. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Local preference notes can retain sensitive personal information if users include it in creative feedback. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid recording sensitive personal details in feedback and review or clear preferences.md when those notes should not be retained. <br>
