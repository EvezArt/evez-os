## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated correlations may be speculative in finance, patent, or threat-analysis contexts. <br>
Mitigation: Validate correlations against primary sources before using them for investments, patent decisions, or threat conclusions. <br>
Risk: Append-only research notes may retain sensitive material if users include it in the workflow. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid storing sensitive material in append-only notes unless the storage location and retention policy are controlled. <br>
Risk: The artifact references an external correlation_engine.py that is not included in the release evidence. <br>
Mitigation: Separately inspect any external implementation before running it. <br>
